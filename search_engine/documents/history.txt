History is the study of past events and human civilizations.
Early history includes ancient Egypt Mesopotamia and the Indus Valley.
The classical period covers Greece and Rome and the development of democracy.
The medieval era involved feudal systems kingdoms and religious influence.
The Renaissance marked a revival of art science and exploration.
The industrial revolution changed manufacturing transportation and society.
Modern history includes world wars globalization and technological change.
Historians analyze primary sources documents and archaeological evidence.
